Company

RTNC is an engineering consultants office able to meet all the investor's design, control and construction management related needs.

RTNC was founded in 1988 by several experienced engineers, in order to aid investors through the know-how obtained over the years. The company relates to the goals and interests of its clients, supporting them throughout the construction process, with rigor and dedication, handling all investments as if they were their own.

RTNC is proud to be independent from all construction companies. The company lasting alliances with its customers are the most complete proof of that independence. Our technical team, working together for a long time, guarantees through efficient coordination an optimal response to the challenges of dealing with contracting authorities and covers all engineering domains.

Efficient and high quality services are the main purposes of RTNC. Based on a continuous search for the best solutions that simultaneously covers technical, financial, environmental and safety aspects. RTNC work is oriented towards controlling construction quality, costs and deadlines.

RTNC main goal is to fulfil all the client demands and requirements. We intended to add value, in a dynamic, competitive and transparent way, in order to consolidate its client successes and making them, with integrity, accountability and competence, our own successes, allowing the client and RTNC to sustainably grow.

ROI

Why RTNC? Check out our Return On Investment.


Deadline

All contracts have an associated term. Failure to comply with this deadline may lead to decreased profitability of the investment.

It is essential to avoid breaching the deadline stipulated in the contract. During construction RTNC constantly reviews the planning among with contractors. It also seeks to give prompt and efficient responses to the difficulties of contractors, avoiding possible delays imputable to the client.

On average, delays in our ventures are 17%.


Extra works

Additional works are the main cause of budget overruns and is therefore the subject of this study.

Before being approved by RTNC, all extra works are analyzed and discussed with the contractor, whether with regard to their quantities, prices or any entailing requests for deadline extensions. As a result of this procedure, the amount of extra work falls on average 35% after the RTNC’s intervention.

The average budgetary overrun in our ventures is 6%.


Retorn on investiment

Return on investment (ROI) can be used to assess the viability of an investment by comparing the expected profits of this investment with the cost associated with its implementation.

This study calculated RTNC’s ROI, provided only by the reviewing of extra works. This is one of the various services listed in RTNC’s contracts.

The average return on RTNC investment is 53%, therefore can be concluded that hiring RTNC is an investment that has return and may even be stated that just for the reviewing of the extra works, RTNC services not only make pay, but also provide client profits.

Remains to analyze the RTNC’s ROI provided by the review and discussion of tender bids, the security provided by the use of RTNC’s tender specifications, the contract budget control due to the various alternative solutions proposed by RTNC, the monitoring and supervision work and consequent improvement of project construction quality, the delays avoided and, not least, the decision support RTNC provides to the client.
